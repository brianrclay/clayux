Next Level Sports

Next Level Sports is a startup that's working on a way to allow wrestling coaches to record matches and keep stats on a mobile device.

Design

iOS App

The Problem

Next Level Sports was just an idea before I started.

The Solution

I created a design for Next Level's first app on iPhone and iPad. I started conducting interviews with wrestling coaches, athletes, and parents of athletes. We then moved into flowcharting and finally on to the high fidelity design. As a cherry on top, I produced on a logo with the help of Mitch Neilson.
