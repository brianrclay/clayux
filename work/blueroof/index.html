Blueroof 360

Blueroof provides a suite of web based applications for real estate agents. Their core products are a website builder and a CRM for managing buying and selling homes. Below are examples of my work.

Design Develop

Design System

The Problem

Blueroof 360's products are currently using widely different designs making the code difficult to maintain and weaking the brand.

The Solution

My team and I are building a design system that can work across all of our products seamlessly. This is still a work in progress, but we are getting close. By the end of the project, the goal is to have one place to get all design assets and front end components. This means not only designing but also developing the components in React and standard HTML/CSS.

Onboarding

The Problem

Onboarding at Blueroof was for the most part a manual process. This lead to errors in accounts and a lot of hours on support reps plugging in repetitive information.

The Solution

I designed and developed the front-end for a simple way for new real estate agents to sign up for Blueroof. The oboarding tool allowed agents to create an account, select a website template, upload their logo, enter payment info and more.
